About Our Church

DOMINION World INTERNATIONAL is a Christ-centered ministry committed to spreading the gospel, raising leaders, and impacting lives worldwide. The church was formed by Pastor Chris Oyakhilome and his wife, Pastor Mrs Esther. The motto of the church is DOMINNATING THE WORLD BY HIS WORD. The church is guided by the Word of God and has maintained strong unity for years.
